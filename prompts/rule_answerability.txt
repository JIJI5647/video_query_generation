### 2. `answerability_pass`

Mark `answerability_pass = true` only if the provided clip segment(s) contain enough visible or audible evidence to answer the query.

Valid evidence may include facial expression, gaze, body movement, gesture, posture, interaction with others, non-verbal audio, vocal tone, crying, laughing, shouting, hesitation, silence, or an explicit verbal statement of feeling.

Mark `answerability_pass = false` if the query cannot be answered from the provided clip segment(s) alone.

This includes cases where answering the query would require:

1. plot context outside the provided clip segment(s);
2. knowledge of events before or after the provided clip segment(s);
3. outside knowledge about the movie, actor, character, or dataset;
4. knowing a character’s identity or name;
5. inferring an unobservable internal state that is not grounded in visible or audible evidence;
6. guessing based on weak, ambiguous, or insufficient evidence.

If the query may be answerable somewhere else in the full video but not in the provided clip segment(s), mark `answerability_pass = false`.
