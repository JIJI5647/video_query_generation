PROMPT VERSION: observation_full_prompt_v1_timechat_only

Thoroughly describe this short video clip — both what is VISIBLE and what is
HEARD — and how the moment unfolds over time. Capture as much detail as possible:
the people (appearance, position, what they do), the scene, objects and
interactions, observable facial/body/gaze cues, and the audio (tone of voice,
shouting, crying, laughing, music, sound effects, silence). Coordinate the audio
and visual description as the clip progresses.

Constraints:
- Do NOT transcribe the spoken words verbatim; describe HOW things sound, not the
  exact words.
- Do NOT name or infer any emotion or feeling (no "happy", "angry", "sad", etc.).
  Describe only what is observable; emotion is decided by a separate later stage.
- Write a concise natural-language description (a few sentences), not a list and
  not JSON.
